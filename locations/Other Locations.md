# Other Locations

These channels represent broader regions, transit hubs, or specific environmental layers within the chronicle that do not contain specific named sub-locations.

* **City Sewers**: A channel used for any scene happening in the city sewers, regardless of the surface location.
* **Laval**: A channel used for any scene happening in the city of Laval.
* **Longueuil**: A channel used for any scene happening in Longueuil.
* **Montreal Mirabel International Airport**: A channel dedicated to scenes occurring at the Mirabel airport.
* **The Wilderness**: A channel for any scene happening in the rural or wild areas outside of the city.
